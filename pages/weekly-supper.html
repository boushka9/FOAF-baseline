How It Works

Welcome to the Supper Club! Here's a clear explanation of how you can enjoy delicious, chef-prepared meals conveniently every week:

  1. Weekly Menu Release:
    • Each week, our new menu is posted on our website's "Supper Club" page and shared on our Instagram profile.
  2. Menu Options:
    • Variety of Options: Our weekly menu features five non-vegetarian meal options and one vegetarian option.
    • Wednesday Special: Option one is always prepared hot and ready for Wednesday evening pickup.
    • Prepped and Packaged Meals: All other options are prepared and packaged with easy cooking instructions for enjoyment throughout the week.
  3. Meal Selection:
    • Choose 1 to 5 meals for 2-3 people or 1 to 5 meals for 4-5 people from our weekly menu.
  4. Ordering Deadline:
    • Place your order by submitting a message via our website's form or Instagram Messenger by 8:30 AM each Monday.
  5. Pickup Day:
    • Meal pickups are scheduled every Wednesday between 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
  6. Invoices and Payment:
    • Invoicing: Your invoice will be sent to you via message or email by Wednesday afternoon.
    • Payment Options: We accept Zelle, Venmo, check, or exact change in cash for payment.
  7. Have questions or ready to place your order?
    • Contact us via:
    • Order Form

      • Instagram
      • Email

    8. We look forward to serving you delicious homemade meals each week!
