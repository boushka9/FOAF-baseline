Breakfast In a Box

We offer delightful breakfast boxes filled with delicious and wholesome items to kickstart your day. Each box is carefully prepared with a variety of delectable treats to satisfy your morning cravings.

What's Included in the Breakfast in a Box:

  1. Cranberry Orange Scones:
    • Indulge in our cranberry orange scones made with fresh cranberries and zesty orange zest. Each scone is topped with a delicate icing drizzle, adding a touch of sweetness to this delightful pastry.
  2. Greek Yogurt Parfaits:
    • Enjoy our creamy Greek yogurt parfaits layered with assorted yogurt flavors and crunchy granola. Our parfaits are a delightful blend of sweetness and texture, perfect for a refreshing start to your day.
  3. Seasonal Fruit:
    • Our breakfast boxes come with a selection of seasonal fruits, carefully chosen for freshness and flavor. Savor a variety of nature's bounty, which may include berries, melons, or citrus depending on the time of year.

Expanded Breakfast Box:

  1. Mini Croissant Sandwiches:
    • Expand your breakfast box by adding mini croissant sandwiches. Choose from ham or turkey paired with cheddar or provolone cheese, along with crisp lettuce for added freshness and flavor.
